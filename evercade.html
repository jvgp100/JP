Resources

Right, well, by 'resources' I really mean, a link to Evercade Info Central. The best Evercade site around, it has the following pages of interest and utility to all existing and aspiring Evercaders:

  • A list of all the cartridges and scans of their original manuals.
  • A databaseof all the available games.
  • A fascinating pie chart breaking down how many games from each platform are available. At time of writing, the NES is the winner with 21%! Wowzers.
